## Maker Comment (post this as the maker right after launch)

Hey Product Hunt! I'm the maker of Locally Uncensored.

I built this because I was tired of juggling Ollama for chat, ComfyUI for images, and yet another tool for video — all with clunky setup processes. I wanted one beautiful app that does it all, runs fully offline, and doesn't tell me what I can or can't ask.

What makes it different:
- It's the only local AI app combining chat + image gen + video gen in a single UI
- One-click setup: the app installs Ollama models and ComfyUI for you
- 25+ built-in personas (from Helpful Assistant to Roast Master)
- Portable mode — run it from a USB drive, no install needed
- MIT licensed, fully open source

It runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux. You just need a decent GPU (6GB+ VRAM for chat, 8GB+ for images/video).

I'd love your feedback — what features would you want to see next? RAG/document chat and audio generation are on the roadmap.

GitHub: https://github.com/PurpleDoubleD/locally-uncensored

## Tips for Maximizing PH Performance

1. **Launch on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday** — highest traffic days
2. **12:01 AM PT start** — you get the full 24-hour voting window
3. **First 4 hours are critical** — front-load your upvotes and comments
4. **Don't ask for upvotes directly** — say "check us out on Product Hunt" (PH penalizes vote-begging)
5. **Reply to every single comment** — PH algorithm rewards engagement
6. **Gallery thumbnail matters most** — make Image 1 irresistible
7. **Short demo video/GIF > walls of text** — people scroll fast
8. **Hunter vs Maker** — if possible, get a well-known PH hunter to submit your product (they have followers who get notified)
